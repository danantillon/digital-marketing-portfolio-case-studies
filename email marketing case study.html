A/B Testing Framework

Test 1: Welcome Email Subject Lines

Hypothesis: Personalized environmental impact will outperform generic welcome
Version A: "Welcome to EcoNest, [First Name]!" (Control)
Version B: "Your first sustainable swap could save 47 plastic bottles, [First Name]"
Success Metric: Open rate
Sample Size: 500 per variant
Test Duration: 7 days

Test 2: Cart Abandonment CTA Copy

Hypothesis: Action-oriented language will drive more clicks than benefit-focused
Version A: "Complete Your Sustainable Swap" (Benefit-focused)
Version B: "Finish Your Order Now" (Action-focused)
Success Metric: Click-through rate
Sample Size: 300 per variant
Test Duration: 14 days

Test 3: Newsletter Format

Hypothesis: Video previews will increase engagement over static images
Version A: Static product images (Control)
Version B: GIF previews of products in use
Success Metric: Click-through rate + time spent reading
Sample Size: 1,000 per variant
Test Duration: 30 days (1 month of newsletters)
