Business Context & Opportunity

Category Selection: Why Home & Garden?

Strategic Category Rationale

High-Research, High-Intent Purchases: Customers extensively research outdoor equipment, patio furniture, and garden supplies before purchasing. By the time they reach Walmart's site, they've done the "heavy lifting" of product selection and are ready to convert.

Mobile Research Behavior: Shoppers often browse Home & Garden products on mobile while at competitors, in showrooms, or planning outdoor projects. This creates immediate conversion opportunities if the mobile experience is optimized.

Seasonal Urgency: Spring/summer seasonality creates time-sensitive purchase decisions where mobile friction can mean lost sales to competitors with better mobile experiences.

Product Page Audit & Optimization Opportunities

Critical Issues Identified

1. Mobile Experience Optimization

  • Issue: Add to cart button 23% smaller on mobile devices
  • Solution: Increase button size by 40% and improve thumb accessibility
  • Expected Impact: +15% mobile conversion rate improvement

2. Product Information Architecture

  • Issue: Key specifications buried below fold, requiring 3+ scrolls
  • Solution: Implement tabbed information design with quick-view specs
  • Expected Impact: +12% overall conversion improvement

3. Social Proof & Trust Signals

  • Issue: Reviews visible only after scrolling, limited photo reviews
  • Solution: Prominent review display with photo carousel integration
  • Expected Impact: +8% conversion rate boost

4. Cross-selling & Bundle Optimization

  • Issue: Related products shown only at page bottom
  • Solution: Strategic "Complete the Project" bundles near purchase decision
  • Expected Impact: +18% average order value increase

Testing Methodology

Confidence Level: 95% statistical significance required before implementation
Test Duration: 3 weeks minimum per test to account for weekly patterns
